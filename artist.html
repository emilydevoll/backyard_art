Medium: Ink - Mixed Media - Pastels - Watercolors

Style: Style - Will - Go - Here

Interested in: Collaborating - Teaching - Learning

Ramona first started drawing in March 2013. She realized pretty quickly that she had a knack for it so she packed up all of her things and moved to Austin in hopes to absorb some of its creative vibes. She has recently started working with watercolors and eventually would like to learn to paint with oils and acrylics.
